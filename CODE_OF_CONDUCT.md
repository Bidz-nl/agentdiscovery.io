# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and constructive experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or perspective.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- being respectful and professional
- giving constructive feedback
- assuming good intent
- focusing discussion on the work and the protocol
- being welcoming to new contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or personal attacks
- insulting or dismissive language
- deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers so the issue can be reviewed and addressed appropriately.
